Company Overview

DJARANETWORK SERVICE PTY LTD is a professional telecommunications infrastructure and network engineering company based in Australia.

We deliver end-to-end connectivity solutions — from underground pit & pipe construction, fibre hauling, blowing, splicing, and testing, to equipment commissioning and system integration. Our expertise spans NBN projects, government facilities, education institutions, commercial developments, and industrial networks.

The name “DJARA”, drawn from Australian Indigenous language, symbolises pathway and connection. We embrace this meaning by connecting people, communities, and opportunities — building the networks of tomorrow, today.

Our Services

DJARA bridges the gap between telecommunication and industrial-grade infrastructure by offering scalable, high-reliability solutions across metro, regional, and remote environments — from the city to the mine, from homes to hyperscale data centres.

Telecommunications Infrastructure Construction

End-to-end fibre optic network deployment for greenfield, brownfield and MDU environments: • FTTC / FTTN / FTTP rollouts • Fibre hauling, blowing and splicing • Pit & pipe installation • NBN, Opticomm, and private fibre networks

Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) & Civil Works

• Trenchless boring for cable and conduit systems • Under-road, rail and river drilling • Poly pipe welding and conduit placement • Civil trenching, pit installations, and reinstatement

Fibre Optic Testing, Fault Response & Maintenance

• OTDR, ILM and LSPM testing • Fibre fault diagnosis and restoration • Live network cutovers and emergency rerouting • Preventive maintenance & 24/7 response support

Mining and Industrial Network Support

• Fibre backbone installation for remote mining operations • High-reliability comms in rugged environments • Radio, CCTV, and SCADA network infrastructure • Solar-powered remote site monitoring systems

Data Centre Infrastructure & Structured Cabling

• High-capacity fibre and copper cabling installations • Rack & cabinet systems, patch panel layouts • Redundant power and cooling coordination • Security access, fire rating, and compliance cabling

Smart Infrastructure & Surveillance Systems

• Smart pole and IoT device deployment • Industrial and civic CCTV network installation • Wireless backhaul & 5G small cell integration • Access control and alarm system installation

Design, Drafting, Compliance & Documentation

• Pit & pipe, fibre, HDD design • As-built plans, GIS mapping, and redline markups • Telstra/NBN compliant documentation • Site surveys and utility detection coordination

Project Management and Site Supervision

• Turnkey project delivery & scheduling • Contractor coordination and site audits • WHS compliance and documentation • Council permits, traffic management, and liaison

Technical Workforce and Labour Supply

• Fibre splicers, testers and field technicians • Excavator, vac truck, HDD operators • Mining-compliant, safety-inducted crews • Short- and long-term workforce deployment
